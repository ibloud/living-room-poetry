# 🛋️ Living Room Poetry (Alpha Prototype)

An interactive, cozy-depressive text-adventure game adapted from the lyrics and cadence of the song **"Living Room Poetry"** by **Trick the Fox** (written and performed by Ren Gill). 

You are trapped in the gravitational pull of your own sofa. Every decision you make—whether it's brewing the perfect PG Tips, binging garbage TV, or dropping snacks on the floor—will either raise your comfort levels, drain your stamina, or rot your brain. Can you survive the afternoon and finally get off the couch?

---

## 🕹️ Play Now
The game is built as a self-contained, zero-dependency `index.html` file. 

* **Host it on Pulsr, Itch.io, or GitHub Pages** with a single click.
* **To play locally:** Simply download the `index.html` file and double-click it to open it in any web browser.

---

## 📊 Gameplay Mechanics
Keep an eye on your dashboard as you navigate the sofa's gravity well:

* **Tea Quality:** Keep your hydration optimal. A splash of milk and two sugars is peak.
* **Comfort:** High comfort keeps you cozy, but too much lethargy makes it harder to leave the living room.
* **Brain Rot:** Binging trash TV ("Made in Chelsea") will rot your mind, while retro movies or gaming keep your brain at a healthy baseline.
